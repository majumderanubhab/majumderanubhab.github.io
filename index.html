Welcome!

I am currently a Research Associate in the Department of Design and Manufacturing at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), Bangalore. I completed my Ph.D. in Product Design and Engineering from the Indian Institute of Science under the supervision of Prof. Amaresh Chakrabarti. My research focuses on conceptual design synthesis, design theory, function modeling, multi-state mechanical devices, creativity, kinematics, and robotics.
Prior to this, I earned an M.Tech. in Mechanical Engineering (Machine Design, Gold Medalist) from IIT (ISM) Dhanbad in 2018 and a B.Tech. in Mechanical Engineering from the Government College of Engineering & Textile Technology, Berhampore, India.

This site provides comprehensive information about my research background, projects, and publications.
Should you require any additional details, please feel free to contact me!
